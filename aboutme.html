About me.

I started learning C# Programming back in early 2018. Around half a year later, I also picked up Unity, as I thought it's the best way for me to practice C# and express myself through my work. Since then, Unity was my main engine, but I occasionally did prototypes in Unreal Engine either for friends or just for fun.
I'm self-taught with everything I do — Gameplay & Graphics programming, Game/Level Design, 3D Modelling/Animating and QA. Sometimes I also mod games for memes or commissions.
